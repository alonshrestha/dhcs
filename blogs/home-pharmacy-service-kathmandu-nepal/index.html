What Is Home Pharmacy Service?

Home pharmacy services refer to the delivery of medications and pharmaceutical care directly to patients' homes which might include:

  • Prescription Medication Delivery - Get your prescriptions delivered instantly.
  • Over-the-Counter Medication Supply - Get access to common medicines like pain killers,vitamins and supplements.
  • Pharmacist Consultation - Consult with a licensed pharmacist digitally or physically.

Benefits of Home Pharmacy Services

Convenience and Time-Saving

In this hectic environment and busy schedules, remembering to bring home your finished medicine is just not convenient for anyone. Especially if the medicine is not available in the local pharmacy and you have to reach major hospitals through the regular traffic jam of Kathmandu. This service is just right for you, saving time and relieving you from stressful travel time.

Improved Access to Healthcare

In some places with lack of pharmacy, especially in lightly populated areas, delivery service can help you get your medicines on time and without much travel. Such people can also gain access to the digitally digital consultations with the pharmacists or doctors.

Personalized Support from Pharmacists

Our home pharmacy services include virtual consultations, where patients can discuss their medication concerns with pharmacists without visiting a physical location or even have your pharmacist arrive to visit you with your medicines.

Increased Safety and Reduced Exposure to Illness

When you receive your medicine at home you don't have to worry about any kind of illness you have or if you are more sensitive to the pollution. Due to the regularly high AQI of Kathmandu valley, sensitive people are better not moving outside so much and this service is just perfect for you.
