About - Doctors House Call Service

Welcome to Doctors House Call Service Nepal!

Our mission is simple: we believe healthcare should always be accessible, convenient, and centered around your well-being. There are times when traveling to a hospital is just not possible, and that's where we come in.

"With Doctors House Call Service, it's as easy as making a simple phone call, and our dedicated, caring doctors will be at your home."

Our services revolve around home healthcare, with a primary focus on:

  • Home Nursing Care
  • Doctors Home Visit and Online Consultation
  • Whole Body Checkup and Lab Sample Collections at Home
  • Home Based Mental and Physical Therapy
  • Online Pharmacy Service
  • Ultrasound Scans (USG) at Home
  • ECG (Electrocardiogram) Test at Home
  • Diet Consultation and Weight Loss Management
  • Emergency Patient Air Transport and Escort

We extend our services across three districts in Nepal:

  • Kathmandu, Nepal 44600
  • Lalitpur, Nepal 44700
  • Bhaktapur, Nepal 44800

Our Story:

Our journey began when COVID-19 hit. We saw a need for healthcare at home, so we jumped into action. Even in the time of pandemic, we were flooded with requests for doctors to visit homes.

We helped over 2000 plus people during this time. Seeing how crucial this service was, we decided to turn it into a proper organization.

In March, 2022, we officially became registered. Since then, we've been providing home healthcare service in Kathmandu, Nepal.

Our Founder:

Dr. Anita, along with her partner Dr. Udagar, is the founder of this organization. Both are MBBS graduates and registered doctors with over 7 years of experience in the field. Their passion lies in providing accessible healthcare to everyone at an affordable price.

Our Team:

Our passionate team of medical professionals is committed to delivering the highest quality home health care services. Your satisfaction is our ultimate goal, and every patient we serve is treated with respect, dignity, and confidentiality.

We are proud to be your partners in providing the best home health care services in Nepal. You can expect compassionate care and a commitment to your health that knows no bounds.

Your well-being is our top priority, and we are here to ensure that you receive the best health care in the comfort of your own home.
